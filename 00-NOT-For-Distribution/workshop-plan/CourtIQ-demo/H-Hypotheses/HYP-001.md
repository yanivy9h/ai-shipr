# HYP — Short drill videos (under 2 min) increase daily completion rate by 40%

## Related Initiative
- [[Initiative: Short-form drill videos for mobile]] — INI-001

## Risk Level
Medium

## Why this might fail
- Players may feel short videos don't give them enough instruction to execute the drill correctly — leads to frustration, not completion
- High completion rate might reflect "watched it" not "did it" — we may be measuring the wrong thing
- The drop-off problem might not be video length — it could be that players lose interest in the drill itself, not the format

## Test Plan
- Method: A/B test — 50% of new users see short-form versions (under 2 min), 50% see original long-form
- Who/where: New users only (existing users excluded to avoid confounding their habits). Mobile only.
- Duration: 14 days

## Metric + Threshold
- Metric: Daily drill completion rate (% of drill sessions that reach the "log completion" step)
- Success threshold: +40% vs. control group
- Failure threshold: Less than +15% vs. control — if lift is below this, short-form is not the lever and we move on
