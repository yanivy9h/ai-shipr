# HYP — Coach-assigned drills drive 3x higher player completion rate vs. self-assigned

## Related Initiative
- [[Initiative: Coach assignment as growth channel]] — INI-003

## Risk Level
Low

## Why this might fail
- Coaches may not adopt the assignment feature even if we build it — WhatsApp is familiar and requires no new behaviour from them
- Players might ignore coach-assigned drills the same way they ignore the WhatsApp messages — the accountability mechanism needs to be visible to both sides
- The 3x lift might only hold for highly motivated players (Ben-type) and not for the median player — average effect smaller than expected

## Test Plan
- Method: Concierge test — manually recruit 5 coaches (Coach David-type) and assign drills to their players through admin tooling (no product build required). Compare completion rates for their players vs. a matched control group of self-directed users.
- Who/where: 5 coaches, ~50 total players. Coaches are manually onboarded and supported by Dana.
- Duration: 21 days

## Metric + Threshold
- Metric: Drill completion rate — % of assigned drills completed within 48 hours
- Success threshold: Coach-assigned completion rate is 3x or higher than self-assigned baseline (baseline: 38%)
- Failure threshold: Less than 2x — if lift is below 2x, coach assignment is not a strong enough behaviour change to justify the build
