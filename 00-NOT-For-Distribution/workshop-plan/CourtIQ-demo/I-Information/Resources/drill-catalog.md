# Drill Catalog

Full catalog of all drills currently in the CourtIQ app. Sourced from the live product.
3 skill categories × 3 levels × 5 drills = **45 drills total**.

Progression: Foundation → Control → Game Speed. Players unlock the next level by completing the current one.

---

## Ball Handling
*Master control and coordination with the basketball*

### Foundation

| Drill | Instructions | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Stationary Dribble | Keep eyes up, dribble at waist height with fingertips | 2 min each hand |
| Crossover Basics | Quick hand switch, stay low, protect the ball | 20 reps |
| Between the Legs | Wide stance, bounce ball through legs smoothly | 15 reps each leg |
| Behind the Back | Wrap ball around your back, maintain control | 15 reps each direction |
| Figure 8 Dribble | Weave ball through legs in figure 8 pattern | 10 complete cycles |

### Control

| Drill | Instructions | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Two-Ball Dribble | Dribble two balls simultaneously, maintain rhythm | 90 seconds |
| Cone Weave | Navigate through cones with controlled dribbles | 5 runs |
| Hesitation Move | Fake direction change, explode forward | 20 reps |
| Spin Move | Plant foot, spin 360 degrees while maintaining dribble | 15 reps each direction |
| In-and-Out Dribble | Fake crossover, keep ball in same hand | 20 reps each hand |

### Game Speed

| Drill | Instructions | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Full Court Speed Dribble | Sprint while maintaining control, push ball ahead | 5 runs |
| Combo Moves | Chain 3 moves together at game speed | 10 combinations |
| Pressure Dribble | Dribble against imaginary defender, protect ball | 2 minutes |
| Change of Pace | Alternate between slow and explosive speeds | 10 transitions |
| Live Game Simulation | Combine all moves in game-like scenarios | 3 min continuous |

## Shooting
*Develop consistent form and accuracy*

### Foundation

| Drill | Instructions | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Form Shooting Close | Perfect form from 3 feet, follow through | 25 makes |
| Elbow Shots | Square shoulders, bend knees, smooth release | 10 makes each elbow |
| Free Throw Routine | Develop consistent pre-shot routine | 20 attempts |
| Bank Shots | Use backboard square, soft touch | 15 makes |
| Baseline Shots | Stay balanced on baseline, high arc | 10 makes each side |

### Control

| Drill | Instructions | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Catch and Shoot | Quick release after catch, feet ready | 20 makes |
| One Dribble Pull-Up | Dribble into shooting position, balance | 15 makes |
| Step Back Shot | Create space with step back, stay on balance | 12 makes |
| Corner Threes | Shortest three-point shot, stay behind line | 10 makes each corner |
| Mid-Range Game | Consistent from 12–15 feet, various spots | 20 makes |

### Game Speed

| Drill | Instructions | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Sprint to Shoot | Sprint to spot, gather quickly, shoot | 15 makes |
| Contested Shots | Shoot with hand in face, maintain form | 12 makes |
| Off Screen Shooting | Come off imaginary screen, quick shot | 15 makes |
| Transition Threes | Pull up in transition, balance on the move | 10 makes |
| Game Situation Shooting | Combine moves and shots in game scenarios | 20 total makes |

## Finishing
*Score consistently around the basket*

### Foundation

| Drill | Instructions | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Layup Fundamentals | Proper footwork, use backboard, soft touch | 10 makes each side |
| Reverse Layup | Go under basket, protect ball with body | 10 makes each side |
| Baby Hook | One-handed shot close to basket, high release | 10 makes each hand |
| Power Layup | Two-foot jump, strong finish through contact | 15 makes |
| Floater Basics | High arc shot over defenders, soft touch | 12 makes |

### Control

| Drill | Instructions | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Euro Step | Two-step lateral move to avoid defender | 10 makes each direction |
| Up and Under | Fake shot, step through, finish | 12 makes |
| Finger Roll | Roll ball off fingertips with English | 10 makes each hand |
| Off-Hand Finishing | Develop weak hand finishing ability | 15 makes (weak hand) |
| Spin Move Finish | Spin past defender, finish with either hand | 10 makes each direction |

### Game Speed

| Drill | Instructions | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Full Speed Layups | Attack basket at full speed, maintain control | 10 makes each side |
| Contact Finishing | Finish through imaginary contact, stay strong | 15 makes |
| Acrobatic Finishes | Adjust mid-air to finish around defenders | 12 makes |
| Fast Break Finishing | Sprint full court, finish at top speed | 8 makes |
| Game Situation Finishing | Combine all finishing moves in game scenarios | 20 total makes |

## Product Notes

- **Skill unlock model**: Users progress linearly — Foundation unlocks Control, Control unlocks Game Speed
- **Completion tracking**: Each drill is logged in `completedDrills[]` array per user
- **Streak tracking**: `streakDays` counter increments for consecutive days with ≥1 drill completed
- **Current library size**: 45 drills across 3 skills and 3 levels
- **Free tier limit**: 20 drills accessible without premium subscription
- **Video format**: YouTube embeds (currently long-form — short-form conversion is active initiative INI-001)
