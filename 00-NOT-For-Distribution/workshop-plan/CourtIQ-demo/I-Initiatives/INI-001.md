# Initiative: Short-form drill videos for mobile

## Strategic Link
- [[ABC-TOM/AI-SHIPR/S-Strategy/Strategic-Bets]]: Bet 1 — Short-form drill videos for phones
- Why this bet now: Session data shows 12-min average session length with sharp drop-off after 4 minutes. Players are abandoning drills before completion. Reformatting to under-2-min videos matches real usage behaviour and should lift WDC.
- First drills reformatted: Ball Handling Foundation — Crossover Basics, Between the Legs, Figure 8 Dribble, Stationary Dribble, Behind the Back. These 5 drills are the highest-traffic entry point for new users and the active arm of EXP-001. Shooting and Finishing to follow if A/B confirms lift.
- Active experiment: [[EXP-001]] — A/B test running since 2026-02-24, day-14 read on 2026-03-10.

## Outcome (must be measurable)
- Metric: Average session length + drill completion rate
- Desired change: Average session length drops below 4 min. Drill completion rate holds or improves vs. baseline (currently 38%).
- Time horizon: Measure at 14 days post-launch

## Linked Hypotheses
- [[HYP-001]] — Short videos increase daily completion rate by 40%
