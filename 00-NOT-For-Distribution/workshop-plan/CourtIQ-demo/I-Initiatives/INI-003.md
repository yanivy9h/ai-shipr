# Initiative: Coach assignment as growth channel

## Strategic Link
- [[ABC-TOM/AI-SHIPR/S-Strategy/Strategic-Bets]]: Bet 3 — Coach assignment as the primary growth channel
- Why this bet now: Paid acquisition is off the table. Coaches are a natural distribution channel — one coach brings 10–15 players to the platform. If coach-assigned players have significantly higher completion and retention rates (hypothesis currently being tested), coaches become our highest-leverage growth mechanism.

## Outcome (must be measurable)
- Metric: Coach-activated players (players whose first drill was coach-assigned)
- Desired change: From 0 to 200 coach-activated players within 90 days of launch
- Time horizon: Q3–Q4 2026
- Secondary metric: 15 active coaches onboarded, each with 8+ assigned players

## Linked Hypotheses
- [[HYP-003]] — Coach-assigned drills drive 3x higher player completion vs. self-assigned

## Decision Needed?
Yes
- Decision: Freemium vs. invite-only for coach accounts?
- Decision by: 2026-03-14
- Decision criteria: Freemium maximises reach and discovery. Invite-only protects quality of early cohort and allows white-glove onboarding. Which matters more at this stage?
- Options considered:
  1. Freemium — coaches sign up self-serve, limited features free, advanced assignment features paid
  2. Invite-only beta — Dana personally recruits 15 coaches, full features free, gathers signal before opening up
  3. Hybrid — open sign-up but manually review and approve each coach account (combines reach with quality control)
