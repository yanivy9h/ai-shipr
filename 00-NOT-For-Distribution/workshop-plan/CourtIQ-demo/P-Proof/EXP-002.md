# EXP-002 — Re-Engagement Notification Language Test

**Linked Hypothesis:** HYP-004 (Language frame drives higher day-3 return rate)
**Linked Initiative:** INI-002 (Progress tracking — visible improvement)
**Status:** Planned

---

## Method

3-arm push notification test — ~33% per arm of all day-3 inactive players (no app open since signup day).

**Arm A — Control (current copy):**
> "Time to practice your basketball drills 🏀"

**Arm B — Achievement frame** *(from INT-001 signal — "beat your own number"):*
> "You're 1 drill away from a 3-day streak. Don't break it."
> *(Players with 0 completions: "Your first streak starts today.")*

**Arm C — Directive frame** *(from INT-002 signal — "just tell me what to do"):*
> "Today's drill: Crossover Basics — takes 3 minutes."
> *(Personalised to last drill in queue; fallback: "Ball Handling Basics")*

---

## Experiment Window

- **Start:** 2026-03-10 (after EXP-001 day-14 read; clears Ron's bandwidth)
- **End:** 2026-03-31 (21 days)
- **Early read:** 2026-03-17 (day 7)

---

## Who's in Test

- New players (Ben-type) who have NOT opened the app since day 1
- Day-3 trigger only (not day-5 or day-7 re-engagement flows)
- iOS and Android, mobile only
- Minimum 200/arm for significance at 80% power
- Excluded: Players who returned organically on days 2–3

---

## Primary Metric

**Day-3 return rate** = % of notified players who open app + complete ≥1 drill within 24 hours of notification

- Control baseline: ~18% (estimated from current analytics)
- Success threshold: ≥+20% lift in winning arm (i.e., ≥21.6%)
- Failure threshold: <+10% across both treatment arms

---

## Secondary Metrics

- **Notification open rate** — measures headline copy impact independent of CTA
- **Drill completion rate on return session** — did they just look, or actually practice?
- **Day-7 return rate** — does the language effect persist beyond the initial trigger?

---

## Risks Being Watched

- Arm B presupposes a streak — players with 0 completions get a fallback copy variant; requires logic in notification service
- Arm C requires knowing the next queued drill; fallback copy needed if queue is empty
- Small cohort risk: if weekly signups drop, arms may not hit 200 minimum by day 21
