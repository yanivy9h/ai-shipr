# PM Profile

## My role in the team
- Responsibilities: Own the product roadmap, run discovery, write specs, align engineering and stakeholders on priorities
- Decision ownership: Feature prioritisation, scope tradeoffs, experiment design. Escalate to CEO on pricing and positioning.
- Interfaces with: Omer (CEO) weekly, Ron (Eng Lead) daily in sprint, Tamar (Head of Product) on strategy, Yael (PMM) on launches

## My biggest challenge right now
Retention drops sharply after week 1 — 69% of new users never come back for a second week. I have three competing hypotheses for why this happens and I can't test all of them at once. I need to figure out which assumption is the riskiest and sequence the experiments intelligently, or I'll waste two sprints chasing the wrong thing.

## Where I spend most of my time
- Cross-functional alignment: keeping Omer, Ron, and Tamar moving in the same direction
- Writing specs and experiment briefs: engineering needs clear requirements, no room for ambiguity
- User interviews: 2–3 per week minimum — right now focused on churn (why parents cancel, why players stop)

## Where I want leverage from AI-SHIPR
- Faster decision framing: I spend too long at the whiteboard before I know what decision I'm actually making
- Better 1:1 prep: meetings with Omer go better when I walk in with a clear ask, not a status update
- Stakeholder-ready outputs: first drafts of decision memos and update emails that sound like me, not like generic AI

## My operating preference
Impact / Risk — I want to move the needle on the metrics that matter and not waste engineering time on things that won't.
