# Constraints

Documents what limits the roadmap — team capacity, technical debt, budget, and org reality.
Agents read this when planning sprints or sequencing initiatives to avoid plans that can't ship.
Update when the team or resources change significantly.

---

## Team / Capacity
- Engineering capacity: 3 engineers (2 full-stack, 1 mobile). 1 sprint = 2 weeks. Mobile is the bottleneck — all player-facing features require the mobile engineer.
- Design capacity: No dedicated designer. PM (Dana) owns design in Figma. Engineering reviews for feasibility.
- PM capacity: Solo PM. Can run one major initiative at a time plus one smaller track.

## Tech / Platform constraints
- Video infrastructure: Current video player is a third-party embed. Rebuilding for short-form requires either a new player or significant backend work to segment existing videos.
- iOS/Android parity: Every mobile feature ships to both platforms. Android lags iOS by ~2 weeks typically.
- No data warehouse yet: Analytics are GA4 + manual Mixpanel queries. Retention metrics require manual calculation — not automated dashboards.

## Business / Org constraints
- Series A conversation expected in 6 months: Need WDC above 6,000 and week-2 retention above 40% before that conversation. Everything that doesn't move these numbers is deprioritised.
- Coach beta is free: No monetisation pressure on the coach track yet, but we need to define the model before we scale it.
- No marketing budget: Growth is organic — word of mouth, coach networks, and SEO. Paid acquisition is not on the table in 2026.
