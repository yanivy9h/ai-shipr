# KPIs

Defines how success is measured — the North Star Metric plus the supporting signals that predict it.
Agents read this for daily briefings, OKR work, and business case framing.
Update each planning cycle.

---

## North Star Metric
- Name: Weekly Drill Completions (WDC)
- Definition: Unique players who complete at least one full drill in a given week
- Current: 3,200
- Target: 8,000 by Q3 2026

## Supporting Metrics

1. Week-2 Retention
   - Definition: % of new users who return and complete a drill in week 2
   - Current: 31%
   - Target: 50% by Q3 2026

2. Coach-Activated Players
   - Definition: Players whose first drill completion was triggered by a coach assignment
   - Current: 0
   - Target: 200 by end of Q2 2026

3. Parent Subscription Renewal Rate
   - Definition: % of monthly subscribers who renew after month 1
   - Current: 58%
   - Target: 75% by Q3 2026

4. Average Streak Length
   - Definition: Average consecutive days with ≥1 drill completed, across all active users
   - Current: 2.1 days
   - Target: 5 days by Q3 2026
   - Note: Streak is tracked per user in the app (`streakDays` field). Currently no streak visibility on the UI — that's the INI-002 progress screen work.
