# Strategic Bets (max 3)

Documents the 1–3 areas where the team is placing its bets this period.
The most-read file in the system — sprint planning, initiative review, OKRs, and business cases all reference it.
Update quarterly or when a bet is confirmed or killed.

---

## Bet 1 — Short-form drill videos for mobile
- Why this matters: Our session data shows average session length of 12 minutes, but completion rates drop sharply after 4 minutes. Players are abandoning drills mid-way. Shorter videos that fit a 10-minute window between school and dinner are the format that matches real usage behaviour.
- Who it serves: Ben — trains solo, short windows, phone in his driveway
- What success looks like: Average session length drops below 4 min while drill completion rate holds or improves. WDC increases as more players finish full drills.
- Time horizon: Q2 2026

## Bet 2 — Progress tracking that shows visible improvement
- Why this matters: Week-2 retention is 31% — we lose 70% of new users before the habit forms. Our hypothesis is that players don't come back because they can't see they're improving. A clear progress signal (streak, skill graph, drill history) creates the pull. Parents who can't see progress are cancelling subscriptions.
- Who it serves: Ben (wants to see he's getting better), Mira (needs evidence the subscription is worth it)
- What success looks like: Week-2 retention improves from 31% to 50%. Parent renewal rate improves from 58% to 75%.
- Time horizon: Q3 2026

## Bet 3 — Coach assignment as the primary growth channel
- Why this matters: Paid acquisition is expensive and unproven for this audience. Coaches are a natural distribution channel — one coach brings 10–15 players. If we make it easy for coaches to assign drills and track completion, they have a reason to recommend CourtIQ. Coach-activated players also have 3x higher completion rates (hypothesis — currently testing).
- Who it serves: Coach David — wants accountability without the WhatsApp chaos
- What success looks like: 15 active coaches onboarded, each with 8+ assigned players. Coach-activated players become our highest-retention cohort.
- Time horizon: Q3–Q4 2026
