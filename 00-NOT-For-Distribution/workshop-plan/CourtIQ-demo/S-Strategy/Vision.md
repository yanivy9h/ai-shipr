# Vision

Defines the product in one sentence and sets the direction for where it's going.
Agents read this to anchor all output to what you're actually building.
Update at a product pivot or quarterly.

---

## Product in one sentence
CourtIQ gives young basketball players access to professional drill videos they can train with on their own and track their improvement over time.

## Vision (2–5 lines)
Be the training partner youth players turn to between coaching sessions — not just a video library, but a system that shows them they're getting better.

Every player who opens CourtIQ should leave the session knowing exactly what they worked on and whether they're improving. That feedback loop is what we're building toward — not content consumption, but measurable skill development.

## What we will NOT do
- Build a full coaching platform or replace live coaching sessions
- Compete on the breadth of sports — basketball focus only, at least through 2026
- Build team management tools (rosters, schedules, game planning) — that's a different product
