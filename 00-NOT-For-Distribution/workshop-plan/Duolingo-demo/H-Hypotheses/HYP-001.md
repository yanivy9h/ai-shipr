# HYP — Offering Streak Repair to free users reduces same-day churn after a streak break by 30%

## Related Initiative
- [[Initiative: Streak Repair for all users]] — INI-001

## Risk Level
Medium

## Why this might fail
- Free users may not value their streak enough to pay for repair — they haven't invested money yet, so the loss feels smaller
- The offer itself might feel manipulative — surfacing a paywall at the moment of failure could increase negative brand sentiment
- The churn might be happening hours after the streak break, not same-day — the timing of the offer matters more than we think

## Test Plan
- Method: A/B test — 50% of free users who break a streak see the Streak Repair offer (gems or $1.99), 50% see the current screen (no offer)
- Who/where: Free users only, global, iOS and Android
- Duration: 21 days

## Metric + Threshold
- Metric: Same-day return rate after a streak break (% of users who open the app again within 24 hours of breaking their streak)
- Success threshold: +30% vs. control group
- Failure threshold: Less than +12% — if lift is below this, the offer is not moving the needle and the churn is happening for a different reason
