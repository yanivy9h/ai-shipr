Inputs for the Learner agent. Articles, research, and references relevant to current strategy and initiatives.

## Retention & Habit Formation

- BJ Fogg — Tiny Habits: the "minimum viable behavior" principle is directly relevant to HYP-004 (reducing daily XP goal for lapsed users)
- Nir Eyal — Hooked: the Hook model maps well to the streak mechanic. Useful for framing why streak anxiety is a designed outcome, not a bug.
- Duolingo's own research blog: published several papers on streak mechanics and notification effectiveness (internal reference)

## Notifications

- Localytics 2024 report: push notification opt-out rates by industry. Mobile gaming averages 18% — Duolingo at 23% is above benchmark.
- Android and iOS notification best practices (platform guidelines): relevant for Trust & Safety review submission for INI-002

## Social Features & Accountability

- Internal cohort study (2025): users with 1+ friend show 2.1x higher D30 retention — this is the data behind Bet 3 and HYP-003
- Finch app case study: social accountability in a wellness app context; shows how friend mechanics can backfire if framed as comparison rather than support
