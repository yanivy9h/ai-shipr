# Initiative: Streak Repair for all users

## Strategic Link
- [[S-Strategy/Strategic-Bets]]: Bet 1 — Streak Repair as a retention rescue, not a monetization tool
- Why this bet now: 39% of users with established streaks break them within 30 days. The majority don't return. We currently offer Streak Repair only to Super Duolingo subscribers. Expanding it to free users at a low-friction price point could rescue a portion of this churn before it becomes permanent. The experiment design is ready. Engineering estimate: 1 sprint.

## Outcome (must be measurable)
- Metric: Same-day return rate after a streak break
- Desired change: +30% vs. users who see no repair offer
- Time horizon: Measure at 21 days post-launch

## Linked Hypotheses
- [[HYP-001]] — Offering Streak Repair to free users reduces same-day churn by 30%

## Decision Needed?
Yes — price point for free users is not locked. Options on the table: (1) free once per month with a gem cost after, (2) $0.99 flat, (3) gems only (no cash). Finance and Design need to align before the experiment ships.

## Open Questions
- Does offering Streak Repair too easily reduce the perceived value of the streak itself? Risk: users stop caring about streaks because they know they can always fix it.
- Should we cap how many times a user can repair per month to preserve streak integrity?
