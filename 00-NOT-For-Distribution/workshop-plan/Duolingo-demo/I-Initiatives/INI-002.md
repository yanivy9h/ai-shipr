# Initiative: Smart notification scheduler

## Strategic Link
- [[S-Strategy/Strategic-Bets]]: Bet 2 — Smarter notifications that users don't turn off
- Why this bet now: Notification opt-out is at 23% and rising — up from 16% two years ago. Every opted-out user is a user we can no longer reach. We send reminders at 8pm regardless of when the user actually learns. Session data shows clear per-user usage patterns. We are not using this signal.

## Outcome (must be measurable)
- Metric: Notification opt-out rate
- Desired change: Drop from 23% to below 17% within 90 days of launch
- Secondary metric: DAU improvement of +15% in the personalized group vs. control
- Time horizon: Q3 2026 (blocked on data science capacity)

## Linked Hypotheses
- [[HYP-002]] — Sending push notifications at historical usage time increases DAU by 15%
