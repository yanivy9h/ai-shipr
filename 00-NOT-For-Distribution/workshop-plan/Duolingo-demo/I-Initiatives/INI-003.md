# Initiative: Friends & social layer MVP

## Strategic Link
- [[S-Strategy/Strategic-Bets]]: Bet 3 — Social accountability as the second retention layer
- Why this bet now: Internal data shows users with at least one friend have 2.1x higher D30 retention (2025 cohort study). We are not building for this. The friends feature exists but is buried — most users don't know it's there. The MVP is not new infrastructure; it's a surfacing and onboarding problem.

## Outcome (must be measurable)
- Metric: D30 retention rate for users who connect with at least one friend in their first week
- Desired change: +8 percentage points vs. users with no friends
- Time horizon: Q4 2026

## Linked Hypotheses
- [[HYP-003]] — Users who connect with at least one friend in their first week have 2x higher D30 retention
