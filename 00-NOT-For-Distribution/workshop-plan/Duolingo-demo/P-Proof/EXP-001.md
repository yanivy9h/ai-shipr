- [[HYP-001]] — Offering Streak Repair to free users reduces same-day churn by 30%

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Type | A/B test |
| Test group | Free users who break a streak — see Streak Repair offer screen |
| Control group | Free users who break a streak — see current screen (no offer) |

## What we're testing
Whether showing a Streak Repair offer to free users at the moment they break a streak increases their likelihood of returning to the app within 24 hours.

## Success Criteria
- Primary: Same-day return rate +30% vs. control
- Failure threshold: Less than +12% lift — initiative is paused and team investigates alternative churn triggers
