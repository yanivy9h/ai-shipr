# EXP-002 — Social prompt on day 2 (Friends feature)

## Linked Initiative
- [[INI-003]] — Friends & social layer MVP

## Linked Hypothesis
- [[HYP-003]] — Users who connect with at least one friend in their first week have 2x higher D30 retention

## Experiment Design

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Type | A/B test |
| Test group | New users — see "Find friends on Duolingo" prompt on day 2 |
| Control group | New users — no prompt (friends feature still accessible in settings) |
| Audience | New users globally, iOS and Android |
| Duration | 30 days (measuring D30 retention) |
| Start date | 2026-03-10 |
| D30 read | 2026-04-10 |

## What we're testing
Whether proactively surfacing the friends feature on day 2 of onboarding increases D30 retention — by driving at least one friend connection in the first week.

## Success Criteria
- Primary: D30 retention +8 percentage points for prompted users who connect with at least one friend
- Secondary: Friend connection rate within first 7 days (test group vs. control)
- Failure threshold: Less than +3 percentage points D30 lift — social prompt alone is not the lever

## Current Status
Testing — launched 2026-03-10
