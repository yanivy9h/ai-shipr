# Constraints

Factors that limit what can be built, changed, or shipped this period.
Agents reference this before generating scope or sequencing recommendations.

## Engineering capacity
- 4 engineers on the Habits & Retention team. 2 are senior, 2 mid-level.
- One senior engineer (Lior) is partially allocated to a platform migration through end of Q2. Effectively 3.5 FTE for new feature work.

## Data constraints
- Notification timing personalization requires a new ML model. Data science team has capacity to support starting Q3 — not available for Q2 work.
- A/B testing infrastructure supports up to 4 concurrent experiments. Currently running 2 (EXP-001, EXP-002). Max 2 more before end of Q2.

## Policy constraints
- Any change to push notification frequency must be reviewed by the Trust & Safety team. 2-week review cycle. Build this into any notification sprint timeline.
- Streak Repair pricing changes require Finance sign-off if expanding beyond the current subscriber base.

## Strategic constraints
- Social features must not create any pressure or comparison mechanics that could trigger negative body image or anxiety. This is a company-wide sensitivity. Any social feature needs a design review with the wellness advisory group before shipping.
