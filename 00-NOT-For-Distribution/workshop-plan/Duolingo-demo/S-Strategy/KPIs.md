# KPIs

Defines how success is measured — the North Star Metric plus the supporting signals that predict it.
Agents read this for daily briefings, OKR work, and business case framing.
Update each planning cycle.

---

## North Star Metric
- Name: DAU (Daily Active Users)
- Definition: Unique users who complete at least one lesson in a given day
- Current: 40.2M
- Target: 48M by Q4 2026

## Supporting Metrics

1. D7 Retention
   - Definition: % of new users who complete a lesson on day 7 after signup
   - Current: 38%
   - Target: 46% by Q3 2026

2. Streak Continuation Rate
   - Definition: % of users with a 7+ day streak who maintain it over the following 30 days
   - Current: 61%
   - Target: 72% by Q3 2026

3. Notification Opt-out Rate
   - Definition: % of users who have disabled push notifications
   - Current: 23%
   - Target: Below 17% by Q3 2026
   - Note: This is trending in the wrong direction. Was 16% two years ago. Every percentage point here is DAU lost.

4. Streak Repair Conversion Rate
   - Definition: % of users who see the Streak Repair offer after breaking a streak and complete payment
   - Current: 11%
   - Target: 22% by Q2 2026
   - Note: Currently only shown to Super Duolingo subscribers. Testing expansion to free users is INI-001.
