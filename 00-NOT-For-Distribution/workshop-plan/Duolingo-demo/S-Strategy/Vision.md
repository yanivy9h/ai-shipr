# Vision

Defines the product in one sentence and sets the direction for where it's going.
Agents read this to anchor all output to what you're actually building.
Update at a product pivot or quarterly.

---

## Product in one sentence
Duolingo makes language learning a daily habit — through streaks, gamification, and short lessons that fit into 5 minutes anywhere.

## Vision (2–5 lines)
Be the first thing 100 million people do every morning — before coffee, before email, before anything else.

The product works when learning feels less like studying and more like a game you're winning. Every feature decision maps back to one question: does this make someone more likely to open Duolingo tomorrow? Retention is the product. Everything else is in service of that.

## What we will NOT do
- Replace classroom or professional language instruction
- Build long-form content (30+ minute lessons) — short sessions are a core design principle
- Compete on credentials or certification — that's a different user need
- Build enterprise tools in this team — that's a separate product line
