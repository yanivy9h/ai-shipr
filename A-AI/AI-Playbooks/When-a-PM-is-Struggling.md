# Playbook: When a PM is Struggling

**Paste this into Claude Code when a PM on your team is underperforming, stuck, or showing distress signals.**
Use it before a difficult conversation — not after it.

This playbook helps you understand what's driving the situation, structure your approach, and prepare what to say.

---

## Instructions to Claude

Read the following files before responding:
- `R-Relationships/Team/PM-Profiles/[PM-Name].md`
- `R-Relationships/Team/1on1-Log/[PM-Name]-log.md`
- All files in `I-Initiatives/` where this PM is the owner (scan for status, blockers, decisions overdue)
- `A-AI/AI-Agents/Team-Manager.md`

Then:

**Step 1 — Situation Read**
From the profile and 1:1 log, surface:
- How long has this pattern been visible in the data?
- Is this new or recurring?
- What has already been tried (from the log)?
- What does their initiative state suggest — overload, wrong fit, unclear direction, or skill gap?

**Step 2 — Run Team-Manager (Mode 2: PM is Struggling)**
Diagnose the likely root cause and produce 2-3 approaches with tradeoffs.

**Step 3 — Conversation Framework**
Produce specific language for the next conversation with this PM:
- How to open (without ambiguity about purpose)
- The core message (specific, behavioral, not a character judgment)
- What change is expected and by when
- Anticipated response and how to hold the position

**Step 4 — Follow-Up Plan**
What to watch for in the next 2 weeks and when to escalate if nothing changes.

**Step 5 — Log**
After the conversation, come back and log what happened to:
- `R-Relationships/Team/1on1-Log/[PM-Name]-log.md`
- `R-Relationships/Team/PM-Profiles/[PM-Name].md` (coaching themes section)
