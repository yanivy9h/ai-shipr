# Teams Channels to Watch

Defines which channels the /today workflow reads from when `teams: active` in Settings.md.
Only threads from channels listed here are surfaced in the daily briefing.

---

## Channels

| Channel | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| [General] | Company-wide decisions |
| [Product] | Product team updates, priorities |
| [Stakeholders] | Alignment signals, asks, escalations |

---

## How to update

Add or remove rows above. Activate Teams in Settings.md (`teams: active`) to start using this feed.
