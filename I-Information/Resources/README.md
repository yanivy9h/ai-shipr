# Resources

Raw inputs you want AI-SHIPR to learn from, reference, or surface during your work.

This is the system's reading pile. Drop things here and the Learner Agent will process them.

| File | What it contains |
|---|---|
| `pm-resources.md` | Curated list of AI + PM newsletters and resources — currently subscribed and recommended. Last updated: 2026-03-04 |

## What belongs here

- Articles and research about your market, users, or competitors
- PM frameworks and methodologies you find valuable
- User interview notes or research summaries (before moving to P-Proof)
- Industry reports
- Competitor product observations
- Books or essays you want the system to have context from
- Your own past retrospectives, post-mortems, or write-ups

---

## How the Learner Agent uses this

When triggered, the Learner scans for new or unprocessed files,
extracts key insights, and adds a summary to Learning.md or the relevant agent context.
Files it has processed are marked with a frontmatter flag: `processed: true`

---

## Naming convention

Use descriptive names: `user-research-q1-2026.md`, `competitor-analysis-notion.md`, `article-shape-up-summary.md`
