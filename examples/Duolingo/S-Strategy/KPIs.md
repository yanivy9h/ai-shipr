# KPIs

Defines how success is measured — the North Star Metric plus the supporting signals that predict it.
Agents read this for daily briefings, OKR work, and business case framing.
Update each planning cycle.

---

## North Star Metric
- Name: Daily Active Users (DAU)
- Definition: Unique users who complete at least one lesson on a given day
- Current: 40.2M (company-wide, Q1 2026)
- Team target: Learner Engagement team owns D14 Retention as the leading indicator — DAU at our scale is a lagging outcome, not a team lever.

## Supporting Metrics

1. D14 Retention
   - Definition: % of new users who complete at least one lesson on day 14 (not day 1–13, specifically day 14)
   - Current: 22%
   - Target: 30% by Q3 2026
   - Why this one: Day 14 is the inflection point — users who reach day 14 with an active streak have a 70% chance of being active at day 90.

2. Streak Adoption Rate
   - Definition: % of users with a 7-day streak by their 14th day in the app
   - Current: 18%
   - Target: 28% by Q3 2026
   - Note: A user with a 7-day streak by day 14 is worth 4x a user without one in terms of 90-day LTV.

3. Push Notification Opt-In Rate
   - Definition: % of new users who enable push notifications and have not disabled them by day 7
   - Current: 54%
   - Target: 65% by Q2 2026
   - Note: Opt-in rate dropped 6 points after iOS permission prompt change in 2025. Recovery is a priority.

4. Streak-Loss Churn Rate
   - Definition: % of users who lose their first streak and do not open the app in the following 7 days
   - Current: 38%
   - Target: 25% by Q3 2026
