# Shared Resources

Org-wide research, market context, and reference material that all PMs should have access to.
Drop things here that are relevant to the whole product organization — not just one product.

---

## What belongs here

- **Competitor analysis** — org-level competitive landscape docs
- **Market research** — company-commissioned or curated market reports
- **User research** — studies that cut across products (e.g. company-wide user survey, NPS analysis)
- **Company positioning docs** — how the org describes itself and its products
- **Industry reports** — research relevant to the company's space
- **Post-mortems** — notable org-level incidents or retrospectives worth preserving
- **Any research document used by more than one PM**

---

## What does NOT belong here

PM-specific reading lists, personal notes, or product-specific research stay in `pm-[name]/I-Information/Resources/`.

---

## Naming convention

Use descriptive names that include scope and date:
`competitor-analysis-[competitor]-[YYYY-MM].md`
`market-report-[topic]-[YYYY].md`
`user-research-company-wide-[YYYY-QQ].md`
